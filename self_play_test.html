Instructions

Great job! You have finished the tutorial. Now, you are ready to enter the robot factory and complete actual builder and describer tasks.

Because the robots are very good at their jobs, they will know quickly that you are a human if you give bad descriptions and do not interpret their descriptions well.

You will complete a total of 6 tasks. You will recieve AMOUNT for completing the study, and a bonus of AMOUNT for each successful communication (either you correctly interpret a description, or you write a description that is correctly interpreted).
